1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Flux Gravity ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and processes personal data when you use the mobile game.

We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable laws.

2. Data Controller

Email: fluxgravity.dev@gmail.com

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data, including:

  • Game events (e.g. game start, game over)
  • Scores and progress
  • In-game actions (e.g. purchases, settings changes)
  • Ad interactions
Purpose

Improve gameplay, balance, and user experience

Legal basis (EU)

Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent)

3.2 Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display ads. This may involve collecting:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android)
  • IP address
  • Interaction with ads
Purpose

Show ads, including personalized ads (if consent is given)

Legal basis (EU)

Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent)

3.3 In-App Purchases

When you make purchases (e.g. "Remove Ads"):

  • Transaction data is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play
  • Purchase events may be tracked via Firebase Analytics

We do not receive payment details.

Legal basis

Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract performance)

3.4 Consent Management

We use Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to ask for user consent and store consent status.

4. Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and not transmitted:

  • High score
  • Collected shards
  • Unlocked skins/backgrounds
  • Settings (sound, haptics, mute)
  • Ad-removal status
