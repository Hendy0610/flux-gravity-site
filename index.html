FLUX GRAVITY

Privacy Policy · Last updated: May 2026

Flux Gravity ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free arcade game. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

We do not directly collect or store any personally identifiable information. The following third-party services may collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob – serves advertisements. May collect device identifiers and usage data to personalise ads. Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics – collects anonymised in-app event data (e.g. game started, score reached) to help us improve the game. Firebase Privacy Policy

2. Advertising & Tracking (iOS)

On iOS, we request permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before any cross-app tracking occurs. You can change this at any time in Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we will ask for your consent before showing personalised ads. You can withdraw or change your consent at any time via the Privacy Options link on the main menu.

3. Local Storage

The game stores the following data locally on your device only — it is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Your best score
  • Total gravity shards collected
  • Unlocked and equipped skins
  • Mute preference
  • Whether you have purchased "Remove Ads"

4. In-App Purchases

The "Remove Ads" purchase is processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

5. Children's Privacy

Flux Gravity is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
